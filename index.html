About the Band

Organic Sound Machine is a contemporary organ trio based in the heart of San Francisco. We take the classic organ trio format and infuse it with modern sensibilities, playing everything from funky jazz standards to reimagined pop tunes.

Our sound blends traditional Hammond organ tones with contemporary rhythms and arrangements, creating a unique musical experience that honors the past while embracing the present.
